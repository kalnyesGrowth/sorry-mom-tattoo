Care Instructions

Aftercare

Proper aftercare ensures your tattoo or piercing heals beautifully

Tattoo Aftercare

  • Keep bandage on for 2-4 hours after your session
  • Wash gently with fragrance-free soap and lukewarm water
  • Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer 2-3 times daily
  • Do not scratch, pick, or peel any flaking skin
  • Avoid direct sunlight, pools, and hot tubs for 2-3 weeks
  • Wear loose clothing over the tattooed area
  • Contact us if you notice signs of infection

Piercing Aftercare

  • Clean piercing 2 times daily with sterile saline solution
  • Do not rotate, twist, or touch the jewelry unnecessarily
  • Avoid sleeping on the piercing during healing
  • Keep hair products, makeup, and sprays away from the area
  • Do not remove jewelry during the healing period
  • Healing time varies: earlobes 6-8 weeks, cartilage 3-6 months
  • Come back if you have any concerns about healing

Questions

FAQ

How much does a tattoo cost?
Our hourly rates range from $100 to $150 depending on the style and placement. There is a $100 minimum per session and a $50 deposit required to book. The deposit goes toward the final cost of your tattoo. For a more accurate estimate, call us or come in for a free consultation.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes, we welcome walk-ins Monday through Saturday from 12 PM to 8 PM and Sundays from 12 PM to 6 PM. Availability depends on our artists' schedules, so calling ahead is always a good idea for larger pieces. Small tattoos and piercings can usually be done same-day.
Does getting a tattoo hurt?
Pain varies by person and placement. Areas with more bone or nerve endings (ribs, spine, inner arm) tend to be more sensitive. Most clients describe the feeling as a constant scratching or vibrating sensation. Our artists work at a comfortable pace and are happy to take breaks when needed.
How should I prepare for my appointment?
Get a good night's sleep, eat a solid meal before your session, and stay hydrated. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before (it thins the blood). Wear comfortable, loose clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed. Bring a valid photo ID.
What is the deposit policy?
A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to book an appointment. This reserves your time slot and goes toward the cost of your tattoo. If you need to reschedule, please give us at least 24 hours notice to keep your deposit.
How old do I need to be?
You must be 18 years of age or older with a valid photo ID to get a tattoo in Virginia. For piercings, minors may be eligible with parental consent. A parent or legal guardian must be present with valid ID.
Do you do touch-ups?
Yes. If your tattoo needs a touch-up due to normal healing, we offer complimentary touch-ups for work done at our shop. Just contact us to schedule a follow-up appointment once your tattoo is fully healed (typically 4-6 weeks).
Can I bring my own design?
Absolutely. Bring in reference images, sketches, or ideas. Our artists will work with you to refine the design and make sure it translates well as a tattoo. Custom work is one of our specialties, and the consultation process is free.
