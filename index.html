Post-Session Care

Aftercare

Tattoo Care

  • Leave bandage on for 2-4 hours after your session
  • Wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap
  • Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer 2-3 times daily
  • Avoid direct sunlight, pools, and hot tubs for 2-3 weeks
  • Do not pick or scratch at peeling skin
  • Full healing takes 2-4 weeks depending on size and placement

Piercing Care

  • Clean with sterile saline solution 2 times daily
  • Avoid touching with unwashed hands
  • Do not twist, turn, or remove jewelry during healing
  • Avoid sleeping on fresh piercings when possible
  • Keep hair products and makeup away from the area
  • Healing time varies: 6 weeks to 12 months by location

Common Questions

FAQ

How much does a tattoo cost?

Our minimum is $100 per tattoo. Hourly rates range from $100-$150 depending on style, complexity, and placement. Bring your idea to a consultation and we'll give you a clear quote before any work begins.

Do I need an appointment?

Tattoos require an appointment with a $50 deposit. Piercings are walk-in only, no appointment needed. Just come in during business hours.

Does it hurt?

Pain varies by location and person. Most clients describe it as a scratching or vibrating sensation. Areas with more nerve endings (ribs, feet, inner arm) tend to be more sensitive. Our artists work at a comfortable pace and can take breaks as needed.

How do I prepare for my session?

Get a good night's sleep, eat a full meal before your appointment, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours. Wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely. Bring reference images, sketches, or written descriptions. Our artists will work with you to refine the design and make sure it translates well as a tattoo. Custom design is included in your session.

What's the minimum age?

You must be 18+ for a tattoo in Virginia. Minors can get piercings with a parent or legal guardian present who has valid photo ID.

How long does healing take?

Most tattoos heal in 2-4 weeks on the surface. Full healing beneath the skin takes about 2-3 months. Follow the aftercare instructions we provide and your tattoo will heal clean and vibrant.

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes. Cover-ups are one of our specialties. Bring in your existing tattoo for a consultation and we'll discuss options for transforming it into something you love.
